Privacy Policy

Purpose

The Privacy Policy is designed to ensure Computer Force respects the personal information of its customers that it collects including those characteristics that identify you as an individual. The policy is designed to ensure compliance with the Privacy Act 1988, its amendments and the associated 13 Australian Privacy Principles (APP).

Objectives

This policy is designed to ensure compliance with Federal and State/Territory privacy legislation.

Scope

This policy extends to all employees of Computer Force, its customers, and members of the general public who interact with Computer Force.

Policy

Computer Force is committed to protecting personal information and will take all reasonable steps to secure the personal information it holds.

Types of personal information collected

Computer Force collects the following information from its customers:

  • Full Name
  • Date of birth – certain software may have age restrictions so this must be known
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Street address, suburb, state and postcode
  • Payment/order history – details about their orders must be known in the case orders are not delivered, complaints, requests for refunds, etc.

How personal information is collected and held

Computer Force can collect personal information using the following methods:

  • Computer Force website
  • Emails to Computer Force staff
  • Verbal conversations had with Computer Force staff

Personal information is stored on Computer Force's secure database which only allows Computer Force staff members with appropriate authority* to access this information through our Customer Relationship Management (CMS) software.

* Accounts Receivable staff, IT staff and managers

Purposes for handling personal information

Computer Force handles your personal information to provide services to customers, to allow for the administration of this information, and to provide relevant information on products that we sell. This can include:

  • Allowing the completion of sales orders you have made with Computer Force
  • Providing information about the products and services Computer Force sells
  • To ensure your personal information is kept up to date
  • To respond to feedback, queries, or concerns you may have

Procedures

Procedures for access, correction and feedback

Customers and employees have the right to access their personal information held in Computer Force systems.

Access via the Computer Force website:

  1. Register for an online account using the Member link
  2. Access My Profile link
  3. Select the Edit icon
  4. Modify personal information
  5. Select the Save icon
  6. When accessing My Profile, a customer/employee is required to use two-factor authentication

How to make a complaint

Access via the Computer Force website:

  1. Select the Complaint icon
  2. Select the Lodge a complaint icon
  3. Complete the form fields, including complaint details, name, address, and contact number
